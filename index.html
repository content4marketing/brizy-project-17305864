Senior Composer of Original Soundtracks for Films, Games & Brands, specializing in Orchestra, Sound Design, and Music Production.

Composing music for cinema is an art that demands excellence in cinematic soundtracks and high-quality music production. I use the finest sample libraries available, such as Spitfire Audio, renowned for their realistic and pure sounds, combined with authentic acoustic instruments to create unforgettable compositions.

For games, I design soundtracks with adaptive audio and interactive music, crafted to provide players with a fully immersive experience, leveraging my expertise in game audio and sound design.

I’m also passionate about creating orchestral arrangements for gospel music, blending advanced orchestration techniques with artistic sensitivity to enrich every project.
